JOLLOF RICE RECIPIE

Description

Jollof rice is a very common Nigerian dish served at almost any kind of event.

Whether, it's a wedding ceremony, birthday, a professional conference, or even just a regular meal at home, you can never go wrong with jollof rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Groundnut oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • Curry

Steps

  1. Get a clean pot ready alongside with your ingredients.
  2. Heat the pot up and add little groundnut oil.
  3. Add some tomato paste and mesh it down with a spoon to liquify it.
  4. Add more water to accommodate the remaining ingredients.
  5. Add the remaining ingredients in adequate proportion.
  6. Stir the contents till it evens out.
  7. Wash your rice and add it to the contents of the pot.
  8. Add some more water to allow for swelling of the rice.
  9. Taste the contents and add more ingredients accordingly.
  10. Leave for some time, allowing the rice to boil, soften, and swell.
  11. Once the water dries up and the rice is soft enough, your jollof rice is ready to be dished.
